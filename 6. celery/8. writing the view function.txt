But a view alone does nothing

A view is just a function sitting in a file. Nothing calls it yet. For it to run, it needs a web address pointing at it — like /fetch/. Right now there's no such address.

Step 2 of 3: give it a URL

This is the part where you tell Django "when someone visits /fetch/, run trigger_fetch." It's the same URL wiring you did in Phases 1–2 — nothing new.

1. A new file weather/urls.py (your app doesn't have one yet)
2. One line in dashboard/urls.py to plug the weather app's URLs in
