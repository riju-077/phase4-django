Imagine a page listing 50 weather cities. For each city you also show its country name (from a related table).

You write normal-looking code. The page loads… but it's slow. Why? You have no idea. It looks fine.

51 queries   ← in RED

51 database trips for one page. The toolbar just revealed a hidden disaster you couldn't see from the code.

51 → 2. The page is instantly fast. And the toolbar proved your fix worked — you watched the number drop with your own eyes.
