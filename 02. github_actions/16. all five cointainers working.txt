  web:
    environment: *django-env       # point at it

Why: worker and beat need the exact same config. I could've pasted it three times — but then you'd have three copies, and one would eventually rot.

That's this morning's lesson, third time today. &django-env defines it, *django-env reuses it. Change it once, all three follow.
